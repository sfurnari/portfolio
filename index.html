About me

I am a Sydney based junior software engineer with an extensive background in the customer service and retail industries. I love working in diverse and supportive teams to achieve greater goals and have a passion for continuous learning with a problem-solving mindset.

After working for more than 10 years in various retail and customer service roles, I grew an interest in how the software we used daily was made and how they streamlined our workflows and created a greater user experience.
This lead me to enrolling in a 12-week Software Engineering Immersive bootcamp through General Assembly where I gained the skills and understanding in web development languages like HTML/CSS, JavaScript and Ruby.

When i'm not working you will often find me hanging out and playing games with my friends online, watching my favourite sports or eating out at one of my many favourite restaurants around the Inner West.
